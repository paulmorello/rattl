How the 2 week challenge works - It's Free!

1

Tell us what you want to work on

Tell us what you want to work towards, this can be anything. You might want to start a business, learn to code or even get fit.

2

Break it down in to smaller tasks

Think about your goal and then break it down into as many small tasks as you can. These will be the tasks that we choose from for the 2 week cycle.

3

Factor in all commitments

Tell us what time you need for work and social commitments. This will help us determine how many hours you realistically have to complete your tasks.

4

Plan the tasks to complete in the next 2 weeks

Now that we know how many hours you have to work on your goal, we can pick tasks that fit in with your availability.

5

We send you reminders and keep you on track

When the 2 week cycle starts, we check in, send you reminders and make sure that you stay on track with your tasks.
